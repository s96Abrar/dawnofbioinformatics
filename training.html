2 Days Long Offline Training on Computer-Aided Drug Design

Embark on a transformative 2-day offline journey into the world of Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD) with our exclusive training program. With only 12 coveted seats available, this immersive experience promises personalized attention and a conducive learning environment. Participants will be equipped with a comprehensive understanding of CADD, from the foundational bioinformatics concepts to advanced computational experiments. The first day lays the groundwork with a basic introduction to CADD, exploring drug design, project design, targeted disease selection, protein and compound identification, and the intricacies of molecular docking. The subsequent day delves deeper into the essence of drug likeness, ADME, toxicity analysis, molecular dynamics simulation, and crucial analytical techniques like MM/PBSA and PCA. Beyond theoretical knowledge, attendees will enjoy hands-on project opportunities, savor delicious meals, and conclude with a valuable electronic certificate of completion. Plus, the addition of seven specific video tutorials enriches the learning experience, ensuring participants leave with a profound understanding and practical skills in the realm of drug design.

Registration Deadline: 6th March 2024
Training Time: Friday & Saturday (10.00 AM - 5.30 PM)
Starting From: 8th March 2024
Location: Dawn of Bioinformatics ltd. Staff Quarter, Demra, Dhaka

Trainers:

  • Sk. Faisal Ahmed (Founder & Chief Executive Officer, Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh)

Training Outcomes:
  • Receive an Electronic Certificate of Completion
  • Enjoy Lunch & Snacks
  • Gain Access to a CADD Project Opportunity
  • Knowledge of various software and databases
  • Learn to Conduct Computational Experiments
  • Additionally, Offer 7 specific video tutorials
What You'll Learn:
Day 1
  • Basic Introduction to Computer-Aided Drug Design
  • Bioinformatics concept on Drug Design
  • Full Project design on CADD
  • How to search and select targeted disease
  • How to identify target protein and compounds library
  • Introduction to different protein & small molecules databases
  • Basic concept on protein & ligand structure
  • Protein & ligand preparation
  • Protein active site identification
  • How to Preform Virtual Screening
  • Basic introduction on Molecular Docking
  • Different types and methods of Molecular Docking
  • Performed Molecular docking with result interoperation
Day 2
  • Basic concept of Drug Likeness
  • Analyzed Drug Likeness properties
  • Introduction of ADME and Toxicity of compounds
  • ADME and Toxicity analysis of target compounds
  • Basic concept on Molecular Dynamics Simulation
  • Different tools and methods of MD Simulation
  • MD Simulation Parameter fixing and system building
  • Molecular Dynamics Simulation Running Processes
  • MD Simulation Figure generation & Result analysis
  • Molecular Mechanics Poisson-Boltzmann Surface Area (MM/PBSA) analysis
  • Principal Component Analysis (PCA) analysis
  • Basic concept of Manuscript Preparation & Referencing
  • Problem solving and QA sessions
