Our Services

Genomics Services

Genomics services encompass a broad spectrum of applications, including whole genome sequencing data analysis, pathogenomics and infectious disease analysis, as well as phylogenetics and evolutionary analysis.

In whole genome sequencing data analysis, comprehensive scrutiny of an individual's genetic blueprint unveils crucial insights into genetic variations, thereby facilitating personalized medicine through tailored treatment plans.

Pathogenomics, on the other hand, delves into the genetic makeup of pathogens, aiding in the understanding of disease transmission patterns, antibiotic resistance mechanisms, and the development of targeted therapies and diagnostic tools.

Moreover, phylogenetic and evolutionary analyses shed light on the intricate relationships between organisms, elucidating evolutionary processes, tracking the spread of pathogens, and informing conservation efforts and agricultural practices.

Through these multifaceted approaches, genomics services are revolutionizing healthcare, epidemiology, and our understanding of the natural world.

  1. Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) Data Analysis
  2. Patho genomics and Infectious Disease Analysis
  3. Phylogenetics and Evolutionary Analysis

Proteomics Services

Proteomics services offer a suite of indispensable tools for understanding protein structure and function. Among these, computer-aided drug design utilizes computational algorithms to expedite drug discovery, while molecular dynamics simulations provide insights into protein behavior. Proteomics data analysis extracts valuable information from large datasets, aiding in the identification of biomarkers and drug targets. Additionally, pharmacoproteomic analysis correlates protein expression with drug response, facilitating personalized medicine. These services collectively drive advancements in drug development and translational research.

  1. Computer-Aided Drug Design
  2. Molecular Dynamics Simulation Service
  3. Proteomics Data Analysis
  4. Pharmacoproteomic Analysis

Metagenomics Services

Metagenomics services offer comprehensive analysis across four main domains: Metagenomic Sequencing Data Analysis facilitates the characterization of microbial genetic material, revealing insights into community composition and functional potential. Microbiome Analysis delves into microbial diversity and interactions within ecosystems or host organisms, informing understanding of human health and disease. Functional Metagenomics focuses on decoding microbial communities' functional capabilities, aiding in the discovery of enzymes and bioactive compounds with industrial applications. Comparative Metagenomics compares microbial communities to identify patterns and associations, offering insights into habitat dynamics and disease states, and aiding in the identification of biomarkers and ecological management strategies.

  1. Metagenomic Sequencing Data Analysis
  2. Microbiome Analysis
  3. Functional Metagenomics
  4. Comparative Metagenomics

Immunoinformatic Services

Immunoinformatic Services leverage computational algorithms and modeling techniques for rapid vaccine design and development. Through Computer-Aided Vaccine Design, these services predict antigenic epitopes, assess immunogenicity, and design immunogens to elicit targeted immune responses against infectious diseases, cancers, and autoimmune disorders. Immunogenicity Prediction utilizes machine learning algorithms to prioritize antigen candidates for experimental validation, streamlining vaccine development and reducing empirical testing needs. Additionally, Immunogenomics Analysis integrates genomic data with immunological knowledge to study immune system genetics, identifying biomarkers for disease diagnosis, prognosis, and therapeutic targets for immune-related disorders.

  1. Computer-Aided Vaccine Design
  2. Immunogenicity Prediction
  3. Immunogenomics Analysis

Transcriptomics Services

Transcriptomics Services encompass various analytical approaches to decipher gene expression patterns and regulatory mechanisms. Transcriptomics Data Analysis involves processing and interpreting large-scale datasets from microarrays, RNA sequencing (RNA-Seq), and single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-Seq), offering insights into gene expression levels, alternative splicing events, and non-coding RNA expression. RNA-Seq Data Analysis focuses specifically on quantifying and characterizing RNA transcripts, enabling researchers to uncover gene regulation complexity and identify disease biomarkers. Single-Cell RNA-Seq Data Analysis provides insights into cellular heterogeneity and transcriptional dynamics within complex tissues, aiding in understanding development and disease progression. Differential Gene Expression Data Analysis compares gene expression levels between conditions, identifying significant genes and pathways associated with specific phenotypes or diseases, thereby facilitating the discovery of biomarkers and potential therapeutic targets.

  1. Transcriptomics Data Analysis
  2. RNA Sequencing (RNA-Seq) Data Analysis
  3. Single-Cell RNA Sequencing (scRNA-Seq) Data Analysis

System Biology Services

Systems Biology Services encompass three key areas of analysis and modeling to understand biological systems comprehensively. Network Analysis involves constructing and analyzing biological networks like protein-protein interaction networks and gene regulatory networks, revealing key nodes and edges and providing insights into cellular signaling and disease mechanisms. Systems Biology Modeling utilizes mathematical and computational models, integrating experimental data and theoretical frameworks to simulate biological processes and predict system behavior, aiding in understanding complex phenomena and guiding interventions. Pathway Analysis systematically examines biological pathways to uncover dysregulated pathways and therapeutic targets, integrating omics data to inform precision medicine approaches and drug development. These services collectively facilitate a deeper understanding of biological systems and support the development of targeted therapies and interventions.

  1. Network Analysis
  2. Systems Biology Modeling
  3. Pathway Analysis

Synthetic Biology Services

Synthetic Biology Services encompass three key areas focused on genetic and protein manipulation for various applications. Genome Engineering involves precise genetic manipulation using tools like CRISPR-Cas9, enabling the creation of genetically modified organisms with tailored traits for biotechnological applications. Protein Engineering utilizes computational tools for designing and optimizing protein structures and functions, crucial for industrial biocatalysis and therapeutic protein development. Pathway Engineering focuses on designing and optimizing metabolic pathways for producing valuable compounds, facilitating the production of biofuels, pharmaceuticals, and biochemicals using renewable resources. These services collectively offer computational solutions to advance synthetic biology research and applications, driving innovation in biotechnology and biomedicine.

  1. Genome Engineering
  2. Protein Engineering
  3. Pathway Engineering
