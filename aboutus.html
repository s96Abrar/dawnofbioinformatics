About Us

Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh marks a pioneering chapter in the country's scientific landscape as the first company dedicated to bridging the gap between complex bioinformatics concepts and practical applications.

This company is a beacon for researchers, with a robust portfolio encompassing genomics, proteomics, metagenomics, immunoinformatic, transcriptomics, systems biology, and synthetic biology services. Originating from the vision of a passionate researcher, it has grown into a vital hub, supporting the pharmaceutical and research sectors in drug and vaccine design.

By fostering educational initiatives like the Bioinformatics Skill Development Scheme, Dawn of Bioinformatics equips students with essential skills and paves the way for international collaborations, expanding horizons for the bioinformatics community in Bangladesh and beyond.

Our Story

The journey of Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh traces back to the earliest days of a determined researcher, SK Faisal Ahmed. His passion for making a meaningful impact in the world led to the inception of a groundbreaking venture that would redefine the landscape of bioinformatics in Bangladesh.

The story unfolds against the backdrop of Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU), where dreams clash with the harsh realities of research in Bangladesh. Faisal's unwavering determination propelled him forward despite the challenges, fueling his ambition to create something extraordinary.

The genesis of Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh can be traced to a small apartment in Dhaka, where dreams took tangible form. With sheer determination and a vision for innovation, he embarked on a journey to establish Bangladesh's first bioinformatics and synthetic biology service provider.

From these humble beginnings, the company emerged, driven by Faisal's relentless commitment to excellence. Today, Dawn of Bioinformatics stands as a beacon of innovation, offering services ranging from drug and vaccine design to genomic analysis.

Committed to nurturing talent, Dawn of Bioinformatics has launched the Bioinformatics Skill Development Scheme, empowering students to embark on their research endeavors. Through this initiative, our aim is to cultivate the next generation of bioinformatics experts, ensuring a bright future for the field in Bangladesh.

As Dawn of Bioinformatics continues to forge partnerships and expand its horizons, we remain steadfast in our commitment to realising our dreams. With each milestone achieved, the company's journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and the boundless potential of human endeavour.

As Faisal envisions a future where dreams take flight, he remains resolute in sharing his story with the world, inspiring others to dare to dream and pursue their passions with unwavering determination. Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited Bangladesh is not just a company; it's a testament to the transformative power of ambition and the limitless possibilities of human ingenuity.
